Chocolate Cake Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
Steps:
  1. Mix ingredients.
  2. Bake for 30 minutes at 180°C.
Creamy Alfredo Pasta

A rich and creamy Alfredo pasta recipe.

Creamy Alfredo Pasta Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
Steps:
  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. In a pan, sauté garlic, add cream and Parmesan, and simmer.
  3. Toss pasta in the sauce and serve.
Paneer Butter Masala

A classic Indian curry made with paneer.

Paneer Butter Masala Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 200g paneer
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Spices: Garam masala, chili powder
Steps:
  1. Cook tomato puree with spices and butter.
  2. Add paneer cubes and simmer.
  3. Stir in fresh cream and serve hot with naan.
